The Endless


The Endless is a place between realities that resides in the sea called Creation. In The Endless, only mortals who possess soul protectors can exist. The Endless is ever expanding, and new realities are born every cycle (every 100 Earth years).


The Endless has two extremities, like a type of heaven and hell, divided into layers. Each layer represents the deeds you commit throughout your life.


The layers consist of an infinite number of realities, all the way from -99 to +99. The positive layers are Peak Divinity, and the negatives are a Lovecraftian Nightmare. Souls inhabit these different layers of realities. After death, your soul either ascends or descends in Creation. At the extreme ends of the layers, Gods exist in each reality. They have the power to destroy realities.


A powerful relic of civilization called the Centurion Guard inhabits The Endless. This group are powerful and death-worshipers. Many consider the Centurion Guard to have been the first-ever civilization in Creation. The Guard protects The Endless from ancient threats.


The majority of the inhabitants of The Endless are supernatural creatures of all shapes and sizes. The most prevalent ones are the Reapers. Each reality has a “Grim Reaper” who resides. They determine which souls ascend or descend.
