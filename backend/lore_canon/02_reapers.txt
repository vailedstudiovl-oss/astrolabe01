________________


The Reaper–Soul Relationship
Reapers are custodians of the soul cycle.
Their duties include:
1. Harvesting Soul Seeds

2. Sorting souls to their proper layer of Creation

3. Preventing lost or corrupted souls from destabilizing realities

Each Reality is assigned a Reaper to maintain this flow of souls and preserve balance.
Without Reapers, souls can become lost, infected, or weaponized, leading to instability across realities.
________________


Souls as Essence
A soul is the condensed record of a mortal life.
It contains:
   * Memory echoes of a life lived

   * The moral weight of one's actions

   * The spiritual energy produced by that existence

When a mortal dies, this essence condenses into a Soul Seed, which becomes visible within the Endless and can be harvested by Reapers.
________________


Souls as Currency
Within the Endless, souls also function as the primary form of currency.
This is because soul seeds contain pure existential energy, making them valuable for many purposes.
They can be traded for:
      * Rare relics

      * Magical artifacts

      * Services between supernatural factions

      * Access to restricted locations or knowledge

For example, Reapers may trade large quantities of Soul Seeds at places such as interdimensional trading hubs.
________________


Why Reapers Collect So Many Souls
Reapers are sometimes tasked with gathering massive quantities of soul seeds.
This serves several purposes:
         * Maintaining the balance of Creation

         * Powering cosmic systems

         * Trading for artifacts or knowledge necessary for maintaining realities

A Reaper’s ability is often judged by how efficiently they can collect and manage souls.
________________


The Danger of Souls
Because souls contain concentrated existence energy, they are highly sought after.
In the wrong hands, they can be used to:
            * Fuel dark rituals

            * Power destructive entities

            * Corrupt entire realities

For this reason, the harvesting and management of souls is tightly tied to the authority of Death and the Reapers.


________________


Soul Contract

A Soul Contract is a binding metaphysical agreement in which a soul — or a portion of its essence — is pledged in exchange for power, protection, service, or knowledge.
Unlike ordinary agreements, a Soul Contract is enforced by the fundamental laws of Creation. Once sealed, it cannot be broken without severe consequences to the soul involved.
________________
How Soul Contracts Work
A Soul Contract typically involves three elements:
1. The Soul
The essence of a being used as collateral or payment.
2. The Terms
The conditions that define what is being exchanged and the obligations of each party.
3. The Binding
A supernatural seal that ties the contract to the soul itself rather than to physical law.
Once bound, the contract becomes part of the soul’s record.
________________
Common Uses of Soul Contracts
Throughout the Endless, Soul Contracts are used for many purposes:
               * Power exchange – gaining abilities beyond natural limits

               * Protection pacts – binding guardians or entities to protect someone

               * Servitude agreements – binding a soul to serve another entity

               * Knowledge transactions – exchanging souls for forbidden knowledge

               * Reality access – gaining passage between restricted layers or worlds

Because souls contain immense existential energy, they are one of the most valuable things a being can offer.
________________
The Role of Reapers with Soul Contracts
Reapers generally do not create Soul Contracts, but they are responsible for recognizing and enforcing them when a contracted soul enters the cycle of death.
If a soul is bound by contract, a Reaper cannot simply process it normally. The soul must instead be delivered according to the contract’s terms.
This ensures that the cosmic order of agreements remains intact.
________________
The Risk of Soul Contracts
Soul Contracts are dangerous because they permanently affect the fate of the soul.
Possible consequences include:
                  * Eternal servitude

                  * Loss of autonomy after death

                  * Transformation into another type of entity

                  * Being consumed as energy

Some contracts are so powerful they can even alter the destiny of entire realities.
