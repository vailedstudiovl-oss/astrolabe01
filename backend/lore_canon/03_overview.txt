DIMENSION LOCK — LORE Overview
PURPOSE
This document defines the cosmology, systems, factions, and realities of Dimension Lock. It is a canon-reference for all writers and artists.
________________


COSMOLOGY
Creation
* Creation is artificial, not divine
* Composed of infinite realities
* Controlled by layered systems
* No new gods may emerge
Levels
* 99 Positive Levels / 99 Negative Levels
* Greater distance from Neutral = greater soul strain
The Endless
* Interstitial space between realities
* Souls degrade here without protection
________________


SOUL SYSTEM
Souls
* Built from emotional components
* Emotional destruction = soul collapse
* Souls rebirth higher/lower based on impact
Soul Seeds
* Byproduct of soul degradation
* Used as fuel, currency, and sustenance
Soul Protectors
* Prevent soul decay in the Endless
* Necklace/emblem form
* Sourced via black market or Centurion Guard
________________


REAPER SYSTEM
Reapers
* Appointed by Death
* One per living reality
* If a Reaper dies, their reality stagnates
Reaper Weapons
* Designed to guide souls, not kill mortals
* More effective in the Endless than realities
