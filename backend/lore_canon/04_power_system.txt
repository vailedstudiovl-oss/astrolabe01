DimensionLock Power System
1. The Four Sources of Power
All abilities in DimensionLock come from four fundamental origins.
1. Biological Power
Source: Species, mutation, or environmental adaptation.
These abilities are innate to a character’s body.
Examples:
* Demon physiology

* Creature abilities like Watchers and Runners

* Alien species traits

Characteristics:
   * Cannot easily be removed

   * Often tied to physical advantages

   * Limited by the creature’s biology

Example in story:
Watchers and Runners appear as naturally aggressive creatures rather than magic users.
________________


2. Technological Power
Source: Devices, relics, or engineered tools.
Technology can replicate or amplify supernatural effects.
Examples:
      * Seedvault devices that store soul seeds

      * dimensional storage belts

      * AI entities like AI Madoria

Technology allows weaker beings to compete with stronger ones.
Example:
Seedvault machines increase a collector’s capacity to hold soul seeds.
________________


3. Spiritual Power
Source: The Endless, souls, and the layer of reality someone exists in.
This is the main supernatural power system.
Spiritual power comes from:
• soul energy
• connection to the Endless
• alignment with reality layers
Every soul sits on a moral scale from -99 to +99.
Categories include:
Rank
	Description
	Commoner
	Minimal impact on the world
	Major
	Significant influence
	Omega
	World-changing individuals
	The stronger the soul, the more energy it contains.
This is why reapers collect soul seeds.
Example:
May is tasked with collecting 10,000 soul seeds.
Soul seeds act like:
         * energy

         * currency

         * fuel

________________


4. Administrative Power
Source: Authority over reality itself.
This is the highest tier of power.
Users of Admin abilities can:
            * alter realities

            * purge souls

            * destroy entities instantly

            * manipulate the Endless

Example:
Death destroys entire hordes instantly with a command.
Admin powers belong to beings like:
               * Death

               * high-tier Reapers

               * gods

               * ancient entities

________________


Power Scaling System
In DimensionLock, power increases through three axes simultaneously.
________________


1. Reality Layer Scaling
Each reality exists between -99 and +99 layers.
                  * Positive layers → divine realities

                  * Negative layers → nightmare realities

Higher layers = stronger existence.
Characters native to higher layers naturally have more power.
________________


2. Soul Density
The more souls a being controls or absorbs, the stronger they become.
Soul energy can:
                     * power weapons

                     * fuel spells

                     * increase durability

                     * act as currency

This is why trading hubs exist for soul seeds.
3. Authority Level
Certain roles grant cosmic authority.
Hierarchy example:
Creatures
Demons
Reapers
High Reapers
Death
Gods
Authority determines what rules you can override.

All power in Creation derives from four fundamental forces.
These are not just categories of abilities — they are forces that shape existence itself.
Force
	Domain
	Description

	Life

	Technological

	Spiritual

Law 1: The Law of Soul Energy
Souls are the fundamental energy of Creation.
Soul Seeds are condensed forms of this energy harvested by reapers.
They function as:
• currency
• power sources
• magical catalysts
• fuel for artifacts
Soul Density
Each soul has an impact level that determines its energy output.
Rank
	Impact
	Commoner
	minimal
	Major
	significant
	Omega
	reality-shaping
	Omega souls are extremely rare and contain vast energy.
This system explains why:
                        * Reapers harvest souls

                        * Trading hubs exist

                        * Soul Seeds power artifacts

________________


Law 2: The Law of Reality Layers
Creation is structured vertically across layers from -99 to +99.
These layers determine the maximum power that can exist within a reality.
Positive Layers
Closer to Peak Divinity
Characteristics:
                           * stable realities

                           * higher spiritual density

                           * divine entities

Negative Layers
Closer to Lovecraftian Nightmare
Characteristics:
                              * unstable physics

                              * corrupted souls

                              * monstrous lifeforms

A being’s strength increases when operating in a layer closer to its native power level.
________________


Law 3: The Law of Authority
Some entities possess authority over reality systems.
Authority allows a being to override natural rules.
Examples:
Rank
	Authority
	Mortals
	none
	Demons
	limited
	Reapers
	soul authority
	High Reapers
	reality administration
	Death
	universal authority
	Gods
	creation authority
	This explains why Death can erase enemies instantly.
________________


Law 4: The Law of Resonance
Objects absorb power from their wielder over time.
Items carried long enough become resonant artifacts.
Example:
Flybutt explains that items owned by a reaper slowly absorb their power.
Resonance explains:
                                 * magical weapons

                                 * relics

                                 * powerful heirlooms

Examples:
                                    * May’s scythe

                                    * her hat

                                    * her necklace

________________


Law 5: The Law of Corruption
When soul energy is corrupted, it becomes Madness.
Madness originates from the entity Tenebris and spreads like a cosmic virus.
Effects:
                                       * reality decay

                                       * empty soul husks

                                       * parasitic creatures

                                       * corrupted reapers

Example:
Death finds souls that have become empty shells.
________________


The Reaper Power System
Reapers use Spiritual + Administrative power.
Their abilities include:
Soul Reaping
Separating a soul from a body.
Soul Direction
Sending souls to the correct layer.
Soul Collection
Harvesting soul seeds.
Reality Cleansing
Purging infected realities.
Example:
May performs a ritual that pulls lost souls into a portal for purification.
________________


Reaper Equipment
Reaper tools are soul-bound artifacts.
Scythes
Primary tool for:
                                          * severing souls

                                          * channeling spiritual energy

                                          * combat

Soul Bags
Contain harvested soul seeds.
Reaper Ships
Travel between realities.
The Power Formula
A character’s power can be described with a simple formula.
Power =
Soul Density
× Reality Layer Compatibility
× Authority Level
+ Biological Traits
+ Technological Augmentation
