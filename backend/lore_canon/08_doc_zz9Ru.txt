Overview (Read to Get Started and Catch Up)
As a reaper-in-training, Maytradalis travels the space in-between realities called the Endless. She and her best buddy Flybutt, a very interesting fly and her great master, a living skeleton, Death will embark on a very challenging journey to find a way to defeat the mysterious entity called the Lurker. Find out how she slowly unveils the mysteries of Creation and the secrets that lie within!

Formatting Notes
1. Formatting was created to standardize our writing documents, processes, story beats, and elements and avoid overlapping
2. The objective of this DLDS Master Guide is to make sure that we have a coherent story that is organized and well-written.
3. All Story elements will have H1 Headings, H2/H3 Headings are sub-elements hyperlinking to their respective documents.
4. Yellow text is a description. Italicized highlighted text with underlines are staff-related notes.
5. Line Breaks have been added for breaks and will be implemented to other documents for proper formatting.[b][c][d]
