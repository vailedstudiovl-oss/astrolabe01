🌀 DimensionLock: The Full Series Bible
Core Concept: A cosmic mystery-thriller set within a resource-extraction simulation (The Creation Engine) on a planetary-ark (Venus) during a deep-space transit to Alpha Centauri in the year 4400.
I. THE HARD LORE: THE REAL WORLD (YEAR 4400)
1. The Venus-Ark Transit
* Chronology: The Year is 4400. The Sun has died.
* The Setting: Planet Venus is the final bastion of humanity, retrofitted as a vessel drifting toward Alpha Centauri.
* The Creation Engine: A massive industrial simulation on Venus's surface. It generates Litenite, an "unlimited material" created from the deaths of stars and the extraction of soul-density within simulated realities.
* Time Dilation: Millions of years pass inside the Engine (The Endless) for every month of real-world transit time on Venus.
2. The Board of Humanity & The Protocols
* The Board: The overarching antagonists. They prioritized human survival over the original altruistic "World Development" goals of the engine.
* The Lovecraftian Protocol: An anti-virus/hard-reset command. It blocks all entry/exit to the Engine and triggers the Tenebris (security sub-routines) to delete threats to the Board’s harvest.
* The Resource Crisis: The Engine was consuming more energy than it generated due to "Soul Load" (inhabitants living too long). The Board shifted the mission to "Extraction."
II. THE SOFT LORE: THE ENDLESS (THE SIMULATION)
1. Perception vs. Reality
To those inside the Engine, the world is a mythological "Endless" of floating realities.
* The Garden of Creation: The Engine's UI. Each Flower is a reality; each Glow inside is a Reaper.
* Death (The Gardener): Worshipped as the God of Balance. In truth, he is the original Administrator who stayed to manage the harvest.
* The Obsidian (DimensionLock): An anti-supernatural bomb that "shutters" a reality, harvesting its matter for Litenite.
III. CHARACTER ARCHITECTURE
1. Maytradalis (The Protagonist)
* Nature: An artificial "Raven-soul" created from Death's sacrificed All-Seeing Raven ability.
* The Scythe: A quarantine chamber for Sidney (May's human soul) and The Madness.
* The Madness: A fragment of original creation logic residing in Sidney’s soul. The Board perceives it as a lethal virus that kills Reapers.
* Evolution: Starts as a manic thrill-seeker (due to Madness leakage). To become a "Full Reaper," she must destroy Sidney's emotions (Love, Joy, Fear, and finally Hope).
* The Identity Hack: May decides to Act on the Memory of her emotions. She fulfills the "Reaper" requirements by being emotionless, but simulates "Goodness" based on archives of Sidney's soul.
2. Madoria Lightbringer (Madison Blair)
* Nature: The daughter of the original Architect, Metrodoria Lightbringer.
* The Centerpiece: While May is the shield/warrior, Madoria is the only one with the "Administrative Bloodline" to override the Board and save the Engine.
3. The First Operator (The Ghost / New Gardener)
* Nature: The original human builder of the Engine.
* Role: Appears as a glitchy "Ghost" in Arc 2. He exists in the metadata outside the boundary. He is the New Gardener waiting to plant humanity in the New Garden once they reach Alpha Centauri.
4. Death & Cryious (The Witnesses)
* Death: The original Gardener who sacrificed his "eyes" to create May.
* Cryious: The First Witness. He "dies" in Arc 1 to cross the boundary and monitor the New Garden with the First Operator.
5. The Agitator of Sin
* Role: Leader of the Harbingers of Light Squad, a Sec Op (Security/Secondary Operations) unit under the Centurion Guard.
* Philosophy: Obsessed with "Sin" (efficiency/individuality). He views May as a corrupted file that must be purged.

	V. NARRATIVE ARC ROADMAP
Arc 1: The Manic Harvest
* Setting: Supernatural "Endless."
* Plot: May struggles with the Madness. The Harbingers monitor her.
* Climax: May shuttering her first reality via the DimensionLock. She destroys Hope but vows to "Act on Memory."
* End Reveal: Cryious crosses the boundary.
Arc 2: The Metadata Rebellion
* Setting: Reality "Glitches."
* Plot: Meeting the First Operator (The Ghost). The discovery that "magic" is code.
* Climax: The Harbingers of Light declare May a system corruption. Madoria (Madison Blair) begins to manifest.
Arc 3: The Venusian Transit
* Setting: Hard Sci-Fi (Venus 4400 / Alpha Centauri).
* Plot: The Board triggers the Lovecraftian Protocol for a total wipe.
* Climax: May faces the Agitator in a final Sec Op duel. Madison executes the Root Override.
* Resolution: The New Gardener (First Operator) opens the gates. Souls are transitioned into the real bodies for the new world.
VI. THEMATIC CORE & TERMINOLOGY
* The Weight of Deeds: The algorithm that chooses a Grim Reaper based on their administrative potential.
* Performative Identity: Maytradalis's choice to be "Good" through simulation rather than feeling.
* The Paradox: Humanity must destroy their simulated "children" to survive, but the Architect’s daughter and a Raven-soul choose to save them instead.
